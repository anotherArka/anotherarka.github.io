Nirapada Ghosh is one of the notable figures in Paschim Medinipur. He is known as a teacher, local pollitical leader, and polymath.

Early Life

Nirapada Ghosh was born in Bagpota, Paschim Medinipur, West Bengal. He had primary education in the Bagpota Primary School, and secondary education in Khirpai High School. After that he got his ISc and BA(honours in history) degree from Midnapore Day College.

Teaching Career

Nirapda Ghosh started as a Headmaster in Bramhajharul High School. He hold that position for around 8 years. After which he joined as an associate teacher at Bagpota High School, where he worked for around 8 years. Following a dispute with the authority in Bagpota High School, he joined as an associate teacher at Tenpur High School. In the beginning he served as the Headmaster-in-charge for 2 3 years.
